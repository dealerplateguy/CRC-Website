Critical 2. Google Business Profile Optimization

Your website means nothing without a fully optimized Google Business Profile (GBP). This is where 60%+ of local collision repair searches convert.

  • Primary category: "Auto Body Shop" + secondary: "Auto Dent Removal Service," "Auto Glass Shop," "Auto Painting"
  • Upload 3-5 photos weekly (before/after repairs, team, facility)
  • Publish GBP posts weekly linking back to blog articles
  • Fill Q&A section with your own FAQ content
  • Set service area to cover all zip codes listed above
  • Actively request reviews (goal: 5+ new reviews/month)

Critical 3. West Lafayette Is Completely Missing

"West Lafayette" appears zero times across all 19 HTML files. A town of 50,000+ people (with Purdue University) just 3 miles away. Must appear on homepage, contact page, service area page, and blog posts.

Critical 4. No og:image on Any Page

When someone shares your site on Facebook, LinkedIn, iMessage, or Slack — there is no preview image. Every page needs og:image. Create a branded 1200×630px social share image with your logo and tagline.

Medium Priority

Medium 9. Missing Twitter Cards

Gallery, Reviews, and Blog Index have no Twitter Card tags at all. Add twitter:card, twitter:title, twitter:description to these pages.

Medium 10. Blog Posts Need More Local Signals

Only 4 of 10 posts mention "Tippecanoe County." Zero mention West Lafayette. Each post's conclusion/CTA should naturally include: "Serving Lafayette, West Lafayette, and all of Tippecanoe County..."

Medium 11. No Internal Linking Between Blog Posts

Blog posts don't link to each other. Add "Related Articles" at the bottom of each post to increase time on site, distribute authority, and help Google understand topical clusters.

Medium 12. Duplicate Estimates Banner Copy

The "Get Your Free Estimate Anytime, Day or Night" section is identical on homepage, services, and contact pages. Customize each slightly for unique content signals.
