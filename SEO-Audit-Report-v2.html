Critical Priority — Must Fix Now

Critical #1 No Real Photos Anywhere on the Site

The gallery page has CSS gradient placeholders instead of actual before/after photos. The about page has no facility photos. No team headshots exist. Google Image Search is a massive traffic source for body shops and you are invisible there.

Photos needed:

  • 8-10 before/after repair photo pairs (phone photos are fine)
  • 3-5 facility/shop floor photos (paint booth, frame machine, front desk)
  • Team headshots (Melinda, Arie, Chance at minimum)
  • Each photo gets a descriptive alt tag: "Honda Accord front bumper collision repair before and after — Bob Rohrman Lafayette IN"

Critical #3 Homepage Schema Has Empty Image Field

The AutoBodyShop schema on the homepage has "image": "" — an empty string. Google sees this and it hurts rich result eligibility. Must be populated with a real image URL.

Critical #4 No hreflang Tags (Spanish Version Invisible to Google)

A full Spanish translation exists at /es/index.html but Google doesn't know about it because no hreflang tags link the English and Spanish versions. Every English page needs:

  • <link rel="alternate" hreflang="en" href="..." />
  • <link rel="alternate" hreflang="es" href=".../es/" />
  • <link rel="alternate" hreflang="x-default" href="..." />

Critical #5 Social Media Links Are Placeholder #

Facebook and Instagram links in every footer point to #. Google sees dead links on every page. Need real social media profile URLs or remove the links entirely.

High Priority

High #6 No Team/Staff Page

Competitors show their people. Melinda, Arie, and Chance are mentioned in reviews but have no team page. A page with names, photos, certifications, and years of experience is a massive E-E-A-T signal (Expertise, Experience, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness).

High #7 No Google Analytics or Search Console

No tracking code visible in the HTML. Without this, you cannot measure traffic, track conversions, monitor keyword rankings, or identify indexing issues.

  • Google Analytics 4 — measurement ID needed (format: G-XXXXXXX)
  • Google Search Console — verification meta tag needed

High #8 Blog Post Hours Are Wrong

All 10 blog posts show "Mon-Fri: 7:30 AM - 5:00 PM" in the top bar. Actual hours are M-Th 7:30-5 | Fri 7:30-6. This NAP inconsistency hurts local SEO rankings.

High #10 No Privacy Policy Page

Footer "Privacy Policy" link goes to #. Google expects real businesses to have this. Required for trust signals and legal compliance.

High #11 BlogPosting Schema Missing Publisher Logo

Google requires a logo property in the BlogPosting publisher schema for rich results. All 10 blog posts are missing it.

Medium Priority

Medium #12 No Breadcrumb Schema on Most Inner Pages

Only Services and Service Area pages have BreadcrumbList schema. All other inner pages should have it for enhanced search result display with breadcrumb trails.

Medium #13 Google Review Link Uses Placeholder Place ID

"Leave Us a Review on Google" link uses ChIJExample — not the real Google Place ID. Need actual Place ID from Google Business Profile.

Medium #14 No WebSite Schema with SearchAction

Adding this to the homepage enables the Google sitelinks search box in branded search results — professional appearance boost.

Medium #15 Individual Review Schema on Reviews Page

Currently only AggregateRating exists. Adding 3-5 individual Review schema objects with actual review text strengthens rich result eligibility.

Medium #16 Homepage Missing og:site_name

Present on inner pages but missing on the homepage. Minor inconsistency.
