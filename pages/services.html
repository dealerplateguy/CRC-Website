Our Collision Repair Services

From minor dings to major collision damage, our I-CAR Gold Certified technicians restore your vehicle to pre-accident condition using state-of-the-art equipment and backed by a lifetime warranty.

Collision Repair

Complete collision repair for all makes and models. We restore your vehicle to manufacturer specifications using OEM and OEM-equivalent parts, ensuring structural integrity and a factory-quality finish.

Paintless Dent Repair

Advanced PDR techniques remove dents, dings, and hail damage without affecting your vehicle's original factory finish. A fast, affordable, and eco-friendly solution for minor damage.

Auto Body Paint & Refinishing

State-of-the-art computerized paint matching technology ensures a seamless, factory-quality finish that's indistinguishable from the original. We use premium waterborne paints for a flawless result.

Frame & Structural Repair

Computerized frame measuring and straightening equipment restores your vehicle's structural integrity to factory specifications. Critical for maintaining crash safety and proper alignment.

Glass Repair & Replacement

Windshield chip repair and full glass replacement for all vehicle types. We recalibrate advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) after replacement and work with your insurance to minimize costs.

Bumper Repair

Expert bumper repair and replacement for front and rear bumpers. We match colors perfectly, restore original fit, and ensure all sensors and parking assist systems are properly recalibrated.

Scratch & Scuff Repair

Professional scratch removal, paint touch-up, and surface restoration services. From minor scuffs to deep key scratches, we restore your vehicle's appearance without requiring a full panel repaint.

Insurance Claims Assistance

We work directly with all major insurance companies to streamline your claim, handle the paperwork, and negotiate on your behalf. Our dedicated advisors guide you through every step of the process.
