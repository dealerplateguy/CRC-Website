What We Fix

Common West Lafayette, Indiana Collision Repairs We Handle

After more than five decades of serving West Lafayette, Indiana and the surrounding area, we've seen just about everything. Here are the repairs we handle most often for drivers coming from West Lafayette and the Purdue campus:

  • Parking lot damage — Dings, dents, and scratches from tight campus lots, apartment complexes, and the busy parking along State Street are the most common issue we see. Our paintless dent repair service is ideal for these.
  • Rear-end collisions — Stop-and-go traffic on US-52, State Street, and busy campus intersections leads to fender benders almost every week. Our bumper repair and collision repair specialists have you covered.
  • Hail damage — Indiana's spring and summer storm season can leave a vehicle covered in dents overnight. We handle hail damage repair for every insurance carrier.
  • Hit-and-run damage — Unfortunately common in dorm and apartment parking lots. We document everything thoroughly to support your claim.
  • Deer collisions — Common on the rural roads surrounding West Lafayette, Indiana, especially at dawn and dusk in fall and early winter.
  • Winter weather damage — Ice, slush, and salt take a toll, and first-time Midwest drivers often slide into curbs or guardrails during early snowstorms. We handle the body, paint, and frame work.

Not sure what you need? Send us photos through our free online estimator and we'll walk you through the options.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often from West Lafayette, Indiana drivers and the Purdue community.

How far is Bob Rohrman Collision Repair from Purdue University?

About 10-15 minutes from Purdue's West Lafayette, Indiana campus. We're located at 3400 National Drive in Lafayette, Indiana, just across the Harrison Bridge via State Street.

Do you work with out-of-state insurance for Purdue students?

Yes. We work with all major insurance companies regardless of what state the policy is from. Many of our customers are Purdue students whose insurance is held by their parents in other states — we handle the paperwork and communicate directly with the carrier.
