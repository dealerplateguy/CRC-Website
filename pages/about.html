About Bob Rohrman Collision Repair Center

Over 50 years of trusted collision repair excellence in Lafayette, Indiana. I-CAR Gold Certified, Acura-Honda ProFirst Certified, and committed to restoring your vehicle to its pre-accident condition.

Our Story

50+ Years of Serving Lafayette

For more than five decades, Bob Rohrman Collision Repair Center has been Lafayette's trusted name in auto body repair. As part of the legendary Bob Rohrman Auto Group — one of the Midwest's largest and most respected automotive dealer networks — we carry forward a legacy of integrity, customer service, and automotive excellence.

What began as a small body shop has grown into a state-of-the-art collision repair facility staffed by I-CAR Gold Certified technicians equipped with the latest technology. Our team handles everything from minor dents and scratches to complex structural repairs, serving all makes and models and working directly with all major insurance companies.

Our commitment to quality is backed by a lifetime warranty on every repair. We invest continuously in training and equipment so that every vehicle that leaves our facility meets or exceeds factory specifications. That's the Rohrman promise.
