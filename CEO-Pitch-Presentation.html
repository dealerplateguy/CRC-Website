How it works: All secondary domains 301-redirect to rohrmanbodyshop.com. Google treats them all as one site, passing any SEO value to our main domain. A single Cloudflare configuration handles this — no separate sites to manage.

Total Year 1 investment: ~$63 for 5 defensive domains. This protects the Rohrman brand for decades to come.
