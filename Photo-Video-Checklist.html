Photo & Video Checklist

Bob Rohrman Collision Repair Center Website — Content Needed for SEO Optimization

1. Before & After Repair Photos Must Have 10-15 sets

Shoot both photos from the SAME angle, distance, and lighting. Landscape orientation (horizontal).

  • Front-end collision repair (before + after) Hood, bumper, fender damage
  • Rear bumper repair (before + after) Dents, cracks, paint damage
  • Side panel / door damage (before + after) Creases, dents, scratches
  • Hail damage PDR results (before + after) Show multiple dents removed
  • Frame damage repair (before + after) Structural damage restored
  • Deep scratch / paint repair (before + after) Key scratches, scuffs
  • Full paint / refinishing job (before + after) Color match, clear coat
  • Glass replacement (before + after) Cracked windshield to new
  • Fender bender repair (before + after) Minor collision damage
  • Bumper sensor / camera area (before + after) Tech + cosmetic repair

2. Facility Photos Must Have 8-10 photos

  • Building exterior with signage visible Wide shot, good lighting, sign legible
  • Main shop floor / repair bays Show scale and professionalism
  • Paint booth (exterior and interior) Clean, well-lit shot
  • Frame straightening equipment Vehicle mounted if possible
  • Computerized measuring system Screen visible if possible
  • Customer waiting area / lounge Show comfort and cleanliness
  • Front desk / check-in area Welcoming first impression
  • Parking lot / vehicle intake area Shows accessibility

3. Team Photos Must Have 6-10 photos

  • Full team group photo Everyone together, matching shirts if possible
  • Individual headshot — Manager/Owner Professional, shoulders up
  • Individual headshots — Lead Technicians Name + role for each person
  • Individual headshots — Estimators/Advisors Name + role for each person
  • Technician working on a vehicle (action shot) Shows expertise in action
  • Estimator reviewing damage with customer Shows customer care
  • I-CAR Gold certifications on the wall Close-up of certificates/plaques
  • Team member using specialized equipment PDR tools, spray gun, etc.
Tip: Get each person's full name, job title, and years of experience. This goes on the About page for E-E-A-T signals (Google's trust ranking factor).

4. Equipment Close-ups High Priority 5-8 photos

  • Paint mixing / color matching station Spectrophotometer, mixing cups
  • Spray gun in the paint booth Action shot mid-spray if possible
  • PDR tools in action Rods, lights, glue pulling
  • Frame rack with vehicle mounted Shows serious capability
  • ADAS calibration setup Camera/sensor calibration targets
  • Welding / panel work in progress Sparks look great in photos
  • Sanding / prep work Shows attention to detail
  • Quality inspection / final check Measuring, checking panel gaps

5. Customer Moments Nice to Have 3-5 photos

  • Happy customer getting keys back at vehicle pickup Get written permission to use their photo
  • Customer and estimator at the front desk Shows welcoming experience
  • Vehicle being detailed / cleaned before delivery Shows the finishing touch

6. Videos Must Have

Shoot horizontal (landscape). Phone camera is fine. Upload to YouTube with descriptive titles including "Lafayette IN."

Shop Tour Video 60-90 sec

  • Walk-through of the full facility showing all capabilities Start at front door, end at delivery bay
  • Brief intro by manager: "Welcome to Bob Rohrman Collision Repair Center..." Builds trust & puts a face to the brand

Repair Process Videos 30-60 sec each

  • Time-lapse of a collision repair (start to finish) Most shareable content type
  • PDR demo — dent removal in real-time Satisfying to watch, great for social
  • Paint matching & spray booth process Color match reveal is compelling
  • Frame straightening in action Shows serious equipment & expertise

Customer Testimonial Videos 30-60 sec each, 3-5 total

  • Customer #1 testimonial Film at pickup when they're happiest. Get permission.
  • Customer #2 testimonial Ask: "What was your experience like?"
  • Customer #3 testimonial Ask: "Would you recommend us?"

FAQ Answer Videos 30-45 sec each

  • "What should I do after a car accident?" Embed on FAQ page + blog post
  • "How does insurance work with body shops?" Embed on insurance claims page
  • "How long does collision repair take?" Embed on collision repair page
  • "Do I have to use the shop my insurance recommends?" Great for organic search queries

7. Photo & Video Tips

  • Always shoot in landscape (horizontal) 16:9 ratio for web & social
  • Use good lighting — natural or all shop lights ON No dark, shadowy shots
  • Clean the area before shooting Tidy shop = professional image
  • Minimum 1200px wide (phone camera is fine) Don't zoom digitally — move closer
  • Include logo/signage in background when possible Brand signal for Google Image search
  • Name files descriptively, not IMG_4382.jpg e.g., collision-repair-before-after-lafayette.jpg
  • For before/after: same angle, distance, lighting Consistency makes the transformation pop
  • Get written photo release from any customers shown Simple verbal + text confirmation works
