Part 2

Google Business Profile: The Biggest Free Win.

The Google Business Profile drives 44% of all local clicks. It appears in the Map Pack (the three businesses with pins at the top of search). Based on public data, our profile is under-optimized and has inconsistencies that are hurting rankings right now.

Current Issues Found (Public Data Review)

Fix Now
Address inconsistency across directories
Our business address appears differently on different sites (e.g., Yelp shows one address, our website another). This “NAP inconsistency” is a primary local ranking signal — Google can’t trust us if it can’t confirm where we actually are.
Fix Now
Low review count
For a 50+ year business, our review count is well below what it should be. We should have 100+ Google reviews. Every additional review improves rankings and conversion rates.
Verify
Services list — likely incomplete
Most shops list only “collision repair.” We should list all 8 services individually (PDR, hail, painting, frame, bumper, glass, insurance claims, etc.) — each one is a separate ranking opportunity.
Verify
Service area targeting
We likely are not listed as serving West Lafayette, Purdue, Tippecanoe County, or the surrounding 9 cities. This is free to update and dramatically expands our reach.
Verify
Photo count
Businesses with 100+ photos get 520% more calls than those with 5 or fewer. We need before/after photos, facility, team, and equipment shots uploaded.
Verify
Google Posts activity
These are like social posts on our listing. Posting 2–3 times per week signals an active business to Google and boosts Map Pack rankings. We likely are not doing this.
Verify
Review response rate
Responding to every review (positive and negative) within 24 hours is a ranking signal. Shops that respond consistently rank higher than those that don’t.
Strong
Business foundation
I-CAR Gold Certification, 50+ years in business, strong review quality (when reviews exist) — these are trust signals Google rewards. We just need to make them visible.

30-Day GBP Optimization Checklist

  • Verify and lock down ONE correct address across all directories
  • Complete every single profile field to 100%
  • Add all 8 services with descriptions and pricing ranges
  • Set service area to cover 45-mile radius (all 11 cities)
  • Upload 30+ high-quality photos (exterior, interior, team, before/afters)
  • Write a keyword-rich business description (750 characters)
  • Enable messaging and set auto-response times
  • Set up Google Posts: 3 per week (offers, tips, before/afters)
  • Fill out the Q&A section with 10 common customer questions
  • Set up review request automation to text customers after pickup
  • Respond to every existing review (even old ones)
  • Claim/fix listings on 50+ other local directories (NAP consistency)
