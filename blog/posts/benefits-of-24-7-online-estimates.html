Why 24/7 Online Photo Estimates Are Changing the Collision Repair Industry

February 28, 2026 10 min read

Picture this scenario. It is 9:30 on a Tuesday night. You just walked out of the grocery store and discovered that someone sideswiped your car in the parking lot. There is a long scrape down the driver's side, the mirror is hanging by its wiring, and the door has a noticeable dent. The other driver is long gone. Your heart sinks.

Now what? If you are like most people, you know you need to get the damage assessed and start the repair process. But every body shop in town closed hours ago. You will have to wait until morning, call around, take time off work, drive to a shop during business hours, and sit in a waiting room while someone looks at your car. That is how the collision repair industry has worked for decades. And frankly, it is a broken process that does not respect your time.

At Bob Rohrman Collision Repair Center in Lafayette, Indiana, we believe there is a better way. That is why we offer 24/7 online photo estimates that let you start the repair process from your phone, right then and there, at 9:30 on a Tuesday night or any other time that works for you.

The Old Way: Why Traditional Estimates Are a Hassle

The traditional collision repair estimate process has not changed much in 50 years. You call a shop during business hours, schedule a time to bring your car in, drive to the shop, wait for a technician to inspect the damage, and then wait some more for them to write up a formal estimate. The whole thing can eat up half a day or more of your time.

For most people, this means one of two things:

  • Taking time off work to visit a body shop during the limited window of Monday through Friday, roughly 8 AM to 5 PM
  • Putting off the repair because life is busy and finding time during business hours feels impossible

Neither option is great. Taking time off costs you money, and delaying repairs can lead to bigger problems. That small crack in your bumper can allow moisture to reach the metal underneath, causing rust. A misaligned panel can let water into your vehicle's cabin. What starts as a minor repair can become a major one if you wait too long.

How 24/7 Online Photo Estimates Work

The concept is simple but powerful. Instead of driving to a body shop during business hours, you use your smartphone to take photos of the damage to your vehicle. You then upload those photos through our secure online estimate tool, along with some basic information about your vehicle and the damage. Our team reviews the photos and provides you with a professional estimate.

That is it. No appointment. No waiting room. No taking time off work. You can do it at 6 AM before your morning coffee, at 11 PM after the kids are in bed, or on a Saturday afternoon while you are watching the Boilermakers game. The process is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

What Photos Do You Need to Submit?

To give you the most accurate estimate possible, we recommend submitting the following:

  • Wide shots showing the overall damaged area from a few feet away
  • Close-up shots of each specific area of damage, such as dents, scratches, and cracked panels
  • A photo of the full vehicle from each side so we can see the car in context
  • Any photos showing damage to lights, mirrors, trim, or other components
  • A photo of your vehicle's VIN plate on the dashboard, visible through the windshield

Good lighting helps tremendously. Natural daylight is ideal, but if you are submitting photos at night, your phone's flash will work. The more detail we can see, the more accurate your estimate will be.

Busy Parents and Families

When you are juggling school drop-offs, work, after-school activities, dinner, and everything else that comes with raising a family, finding time to sit at a body shop during business hours can feel impossible. Online photo estimates let you handle the process during the small pockets of time you do have, like after the kids go to bed or during your lunch break. You do not have to rearrange your whole schedule around a body shop's hours.

People Who Work During Shop Hours

If you work a traditional 9-to-5 job, or really any daytime shift, visiting a body shop means taking time off. That is lost wages or burned PTO for something that should not require your physical presence. With our online estimate tool, you can start the process without missing a minute of work.

Night Shift Workers

Lafayette has a large manufacturing workforce with many people working second and third shifts. If you get off work at midnight or 6 AM, traditional body shop hours are your sleeping hours. Our 24/7 online estimate tool means you can submit your photos at whatever time works for your schedule.

Accidents do not happen on a convenient schedule. They happen on dark country roads at 10 PM, in parking lots on Sunday mornings, and during Friday rush hour. When you are standing next to your damaged vehicle feeling stressed and overwhelmed, the last thing you want to hear is "we can look at it Monday." With our online tool, you can submit photos from the scene or from your driveway that same evening and know that our team will review them promptly.

Does an Online Estimate Replace an In-Person Inspection?

It is important to be transparent about this. An online photo estimate is an excellent starting point, but it is not always the final word. Some damage, especially structural damage or damage hidden behind panels, cannot be fully assessed from photos alone. In those cases, the online estimate gives you a reliable ballpark and gets the process started, but a full in-person inspection may uncover additional repairs that need to be included.

This is true of in-person estimates as well, by the way. Most initial estimates, whether done from photos or in person, are based on visible damage. Hidden damage is commonly discovered once the vehicle is disassembled for repair, which is why the "supplement" process exists in insurance claims.

The key advantage of the online estimate is speed and convenience. It gets you into the system and moving forward immediately, rather than waiting days or even weeks to get an in-person appointment.

How Online Estimates Speed Up the Entire Repair Process

One of the less obvious benefits of submitting an online photo estimate is that it accelerates the entire repair timeline. When you submit photos early, our team can begin planning your repair before you even bring the vehicle in. We can identify the parts that will be needed, check availability, and sometimes even pre-order them. When you do bring the car in, we are already a step ahead, which means less time without your vehicle.

This is especially valuable when insurance is involved. Having a detailed estimate ready can speed up the approval process with your insurance company, getting you from claim to repair to back-on-the-road faster.

Beyond Convenience: Peace of Mind at a Stressful Time

There is a psychological benefit to online estimates that is easy to overlook. After an accident or act of vandalism, you feel out of control. Your routine is disrupted, you are worried about costs, and you do not know how long you will be without your car. Being able to take action immediately, even at 11 PM on a Sunday, gives you back a sense of control. You are not stuck waiting for business hours. You are already moving forward.

That peace of mind matters. And it is something we think about a lot at Bob Rohrman Collision Repair Center. Every part of our process, from the 24/7 estimates to the way we communicate during repairs to our lifetime warranty, is designed to reduce stress and give you confidence that your vehicle is in the right hands.
